Atmosphere

  1. Ask students to indicate how atmosphere was developed in a television show.

  2. Have students use music to create an atmosphere.

  3. Have students use colour to create an atmosphere.

  4. Have students decide what atmosphere is established by a specific tone of voice.

  5. Make tapes of sounds illustrating certain atmospheres.
