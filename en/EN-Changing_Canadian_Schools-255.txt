It is important for teachers to highlight these qualities in their daily
work with students.

We have witnessed relationships form and flourish in the classroom and,
in some cases, extend beyond the boundaries of the school. In order for
this to happen, interaction between students and relationship building
must be modelled and taught. Our experience shows that curriculum and
classroom activities provide the best opportunities for the elements of
peer relationships to fall naturally into place.
