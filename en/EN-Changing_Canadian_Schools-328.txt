_Alene Steinbach_ is a parent of a young woman who has been labelled
mentally handicapped. She is the co-author of the Children Service Review
Project of the New Brunswick Association for Community Living and has
been an active advocate for school integration in her community and
province.

_Julie Stone_ is a School Supervisor based in Woodstock, New Brunswick.
She supports the implementation of integrated school programs in six
school districts and previously worked as a methods and resource teacher
at the elementary school level. She is a Past President of the New
Brunswick Association for Community Living.

_Mary Wilson_ is a Student Services Consultant for School Districts 28
and 29 in Woodstock, New Brunswick. She previously worked as a classroom
teacher and as a methods and resources teacher at the high school level.
